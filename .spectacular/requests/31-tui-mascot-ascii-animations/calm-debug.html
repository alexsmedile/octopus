What "half" and "closed" mean (current rendering)

The blink overlay (applyBlink) modifies the eye-band rows: 

level 0 (open):    row 5 = EE, row 6 = EE  → full 2×2 eye block
level 1 (half):    row 5 = LL, row 6 = EE  → only BOTTOM row has eye
                                            (top of eye covered by lid)
level 2 (closed):  row 5 = LL, row 6 = LL  → no eye visible at all

Click ticks in the timeline to pinpoint which rendering state looks wrong. The info panel shows the exact level at that moment. If level 1 looks like the eye is "blinking up" when you expected it to "blink down" — that's the swap we need to discuss.
